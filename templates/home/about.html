Neural Networks to Predict Stress.
A Virtual Assitant

We live in a world where neural networks have achieved superhuman accuracy in almost every field. It has brought a revolution and I believe it is just the start.
I have seen my friends and even me falling victim to stress, especially in an online world. However, we usually dont have time to see a doc. Thus I designed a Neural Network to classify stress level of a person. So, that if anything serious is brewing it can be nipped in the bud. If successfull this will also make us aware of ourselves in a different way. Also we will get to know the very nature of stress. And probably come up with ways to prevent it.
