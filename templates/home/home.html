Background

In Medical Assitant systems people are opten skeptical of false positives. Thus the general confidence in them is quite low. To combat this issue I have considered 5 modalities to predict stress. Furthermore, depending on the smart watch the owner posseses, he may have acess to a portion of the modalities. Thus, this system is fairly general. If a user gets high stress values across various modalities. It is a sign of consern.
The 5 modalalities are visible on the navbar. Research have proven each of them are closely related to stress. For details on the neural network architecture and training protocol please visit the Readme section of this repo. The model is trained on the WESAD Dataset.
The dataset provides many more modalities like heart beat which will be added later

Preprocessing

The analog WESAD data is sampled at 700Hz, which implies for 1 second we have 700 sample points. I have considered time sequences consisting of a continuous chunk of 1024 samples while training ie this model will give a prediction every 1.4 seconds (1024/700). Since different subjects have different responses on the signal values,I have used min-max normalisation. Furthermore, I have standarized each sequence by z-normalization to ensure uniform scale.
However, while using this site no Preprocessing is required as I have setup the Preprocessing pipelines accordingly.

Instructions To Use the site

To find out your stress level just input signal from anyone of the modalalities available on the navbar sampled at 700Hz and has a minimum length of 1024 samples. And you will get your stress level.
Conditions

  • upload a .csv file or .txt file consisting of your signal data.
  • If you are using .txt make sure the values are tab or space separated.
  • The signal should be sampled at 700Hz.
